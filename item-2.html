Overcast

It's not the weather app you need, but the one you deserve.

Everyone seemed to want the same thing in a weather app; The current weather, and the 5 day forecast, all displayed in a clean, beautiful way. I couldn't seem to find one, so I made my own.

"Overcast's minimal design is immediately attractive. So many weather apps these days try to fit a whole meteorological station into your phone, even though all you want is the current temperature and a five-day forecast. Overcast does that perfectly..." -Jkeopke

Overall, it was a really fun project. I learned a lot; both in design and development. I learned how to design apps in Xcode and how to write a functional app using an API in Swift *pats self on back*.
