Branding Myself

It's the hardest thing I've ever done, but the most rewarding.
Early sketches of some brainstorming I did.

Lot's of them just "weren't me". I had sketched up some nice logos for other things before but there was just too much pressure designing for myself. I wanted it to be the best but nothing was good enough.

Nothing seemed to be going right. I'd had a hard time even settling on a website before. I actually considered asking someone else to do one for me. It was too hard and I just saw too many things wrong with what I was making. Then I opened Illistrator one day and it just came to me.
